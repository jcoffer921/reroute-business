Housing Support for Returning Citizens

Stable housing is the foundation of successful reentry. Explore your rights and available resources.

Know Your Rights

No Discrimination: Landlords cannot refuse to rent solely due to a record when it’s unrelated to tenancy.

Background Checks: Must be relevant and fairly considered. Retaliation for asserting rights is illegal.

File a Complaint: Report discrimination to the Philadelphia Fair Housing Commission.
