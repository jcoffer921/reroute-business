Frequently Asked Questions

Why are job seekers free?
Because access to opportunity should never have a paywall. Our mission is to reduce barriers for justice-impacted individuals, not create new ones.
Are organizations charged?
Not during our MVP phase. We are focused on building a strong community of partners. Future tiers may include optional premium features for enhanced analytics and featured placement.
What makes ReRoute different from Indeed?
We provide pre-qualified users, a dedicated reentry ecosystem, and localized impact tracking. Our platform is built specifically around the needs of fair-chance hiring.
When will pricing change?
Pricing may evolve after our MVP validation and as employer onboarding scales. However, early employer partners lock in the "Founding Rate."
