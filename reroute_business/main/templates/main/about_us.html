Our Mission

At ReRoute, we're driven to reduce recidivism by creating real, lasting pathways to fair-chance employment, personal confidence, and meaningful community contribution. We recognize that the transition from incarceration to freedom is more than a legal release - it's a personal rebirth.

Through access to practical skill development, mental health support, mentorship, supportyive resources, and job-ready tools, we aim to empower individuals not just to reenter society - but to lead in it.

We believe everyone deserves more than a second chance. They deserve a real shot at a future they can be proud of.

Our Team

Meet the Founders

ReRoute is led by a small, mission-locked founding team combining product, brand, and operational leadership. We are working alongside community partners and reentry organizations to build a platform that treats people with dignity, moves deliberately, and keeps opportunity at the center of every decision.

Our Story

ReRoute began with a simple belief: people returning from incarceration deserve more than judgment - they deserve opportunity. Our team is building the platform in close collaboration with community partners in Philadelphia and New Jersey, shaped by what people actually need to restart their lives.

We are early in the journey, and we are serious about getting it right. With each pilot, each resume built, and each partnership formed, we are laying the foundation for a trusted pathway to dignity, work, and long-term stability.
