Our Mission

At ReRoute, we're driven to reduce recidivism by building real, lasting pathways to careers in tech, personal confidence, and community contribution. We recognize that the transition from incarceration to freedom is more than a legal release — it's a personal rebirth.

Through access to hands-on training, mental health support, mentorship, and job-ready tools, we aim to empower individuals not just to reenter society — but to lead in it.

We believe everyone deserves more than a second chance. They deserve a real shot at a future they can be proud of.

Our Story

ReRoute began with a simple belief: people returning from incarceration deserve more than judgment — they deserve opportunity. Our team is building the platform in close collaboration with community partners in Philadelphia and New Jersey, shaped by what people actually need to restart their lives.

We are early in the journey, and we are serious about getting it right. With each pilot, each resume built, and each partnership formed, we are laying the foundation for a trusted pathway to dignity, work, and long-term stability.
